Databricks Bingo

A team training game to learn Databricks features. The Bingo Master reads scenarios aloud — players identify the feature!

How to Play

  1. The Bingo Master reads scenario questions aloud to the group.
  2. After the group discusses, the Master reveals the Databricks feature answer.
  3. Players check if that feature is on their card and mark it.
  4. First player to mark 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) calls "BINGO!"
  5. The Master verifies the claim using the built-in verify tool.
